Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • What are the park's opening hours?

    DreamVista Adventures is open from 9 AM to 9 PM, seven days a week.


  • Are there any age restrictions for the rides?

    Yes, some rides have age and height restrictions for safety reasons. Please check the specific ride information before queueing.


  • Can I bring my own food into the park?

    Guests are not permitted to bring outside food or beverages into the park, with exceptions for dietary needs and baby food.


  • Do you offer discounts for large groups?

    Yes, we offer discounts for groups of 15 or more.


  • What measures are taken for guest safety and security?

    We prioritize the safety and security of our guests with measures including on-site security personnel, regular safety audits, and compliance with all health and safety regulations.
